Oats And Meals

Recipes

Here are a few delicious oats and meal recipes to try:
1. Overnight Oats
- 1/2 cup rolled oats
- 1/2 cup almond milk
- 1/2 mashed banana
- 1 tsp honey
- 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
- A pinch of salt
Mix all the ingredients in a jar and refrigerate overnight.
In the morning, top with your favorite nuts, fruits, or granola.
2. Savory Oatmeal Bowl
- 1/2 cup rolled oats
- 1 cup vegetable broth
- 1/2 cup cooked chickpeas
- 1/4 cup diced tomato
- 1/4 cup chopped spinach
- 1/4 tsp garlic powder
- Salt and pepper to taste
Cook the oats in vegetable broth according to package instructions.
Add garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
Top with chickpeas, diced tomato, and chopped spinach. Serve hot.
3. Berry Smoothie Bowl with Oatmeal
- 1/2 cup rolled oats
- 1/2 cup unsweetened almond milk
- 1 cup frozen mixed berries
- 1/2 banana
- 1/2 tsp honey
- A handful of sliced almonds
Combine the oats and almond milk in a blender and blend until smooth.
Add frozen berries, banana, and honey, and blend again until smooth.
Pour into a bowl and top with sliced almonds.
4. Apple Cinnamon Steel Cut Oatmeal
- 1 cup steel cut oats
- 4 cups water
- 1/2 cup unsweetened applesauce
- 1/4 cup chopped walnuts
- 1/4 cup raisins
- 1 tsp cinnamon
- 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
Bring water to a boil in a saucepan.
Add steel cut oats and reduce heat to low. Cook for 20-25 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the oats are tender and the mixture is thick.
Add applesauce, walnuts, raisins, cinnamon, and vanilla extract.
Stir to combine. Serve hot.
